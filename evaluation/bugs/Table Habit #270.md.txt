**Description**
When deleting a habit, the item does not immediately disappear from the habit list. It remains visible until the list is manually refreshed or revisited.

**Step to Reproduce**

1. Add any habit.
2. Delete the habit.
3. Observe that the deleted habit is still visible in the list until manually refreshed.

**Expected Behavior**
The deleted habit should be removed from the list immediately without requiring manual refresh.
