**Bug Description**  
In version v2.0.0-dev1, when sharing content to MaterialNotes, the content is converted into a JSON-like format: `[{"insert": xxx}]`. This issue does not occur in v1.12.1.

**To Reproduce**  
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Share any content to MaterialNotes.

**Expected Behavior**  
The content should be shared and displayed correctly in MaterialNotes, not in a JSON-like format.
